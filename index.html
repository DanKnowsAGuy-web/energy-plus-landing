Heating and cooling can be half your bill. We start there.

Half a bill deserves a whole prescription. The Exam matches the fix to the equipment you actually run: what's right for a simple on/off compressor is wrong for an inverter system, and some heat should never reach your HVAC at all.

20–55%off the HVAC portion of your bill, combined, and we quote the floor, not the peak.1

  • For simple on/off compressors · the most common systems

    The HVAC optimizer

    Learns how your building runs and shuts the compressor off the moment it stops doing useful work, while the fan keeps delivering the stored cold.

    15–30% less HVAC energy1

  • For aging units · any system type

    The coil treatment

    The waste no wash can reach: compressor oil bakes onto the inside of the coil circuit, year after year. One refrigerant-side treatment lifts it off, for the life of the unit.

    Recovers up to 36% of lost performance1, 12

  • For inverter-type systems

    The solar coil

    Not a solar panel: a solar thermal retrofit that captures the sun's heat directly and puts it to work for the compressor, changing where the energy comes from.

    30–50% more efficient system1

  • For the building itself

    The thermal barrier

    Sprayed or membrane-applied where the diagnosis shows it pays, so the heat never reaches the equipment that would have fought it.

    Blocks up to 18 degrees before it gets in1

The SunCore
